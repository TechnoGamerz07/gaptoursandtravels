JAMMU AND KASHMIR

Jammu & Kashmir, known as "Heaven on Earth," offers breathtaking Himalayan landscapes, iconic houseboats on Dal Lake, and sacred shrines like Vaishno Devi. Top spots include Srinagar, Gulmarg (skiing), Pahalgam, and Sonamarg. Best visited April-June (spring/summer) or October-February (winter), the region is reachable via airports in Srinagar and Jammu.Key Travel InformationBest Time to Visit: Spring and Summer (April–June) for pleasant weather October–February for snow lovers and skiingTop Destinations:Srinagar: Dal Lake houseboats, Mughal Gardens, Shikara rides.Gulmarg: Famous for the Gondola ride and winter sports.Pahalgam: Known as the "Valley of Shepherds" and a base for Amarnath Yatra.Jammu: Known for the Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine and Raghunath Temple.Key Experiences: Trekking, skiing, river rafting, and staying in traditional houseboats.Travel Tips: Carry valid IDs, pack layers for unpredictable weather, and keep cash handy for remote areas.

BEST TIME TO VISIT

The best time to visit Jammu and Kashmir is from March to October for pleasant weather, blooming flowers, and lush green landscapes, while December to February is ideal for snow lovers and winter sports. April is perfect for the Tulip Festival, and September–October offers beautiful autumn foliage..
